FinTech Investment & Incubation

FinTech 2.0 Incubator

CONTACT US APPLY NOW

JadeValue is a Fintech Incubator located in the heart of the financial center in Shanghai, Lujiazui.We provide promising Fintech startups with the capital, talent and network they need to reach their next level. With a stunning view over the Huangpu River, our large incubator space is an innovation, learning and idea exchange platform, where startups, investors, and the global FinTech network can interact.

+

Investment

JadeValue invests between $150-500K for equity in Fintech startups, mainly from Angel to Pre-A round.We are looking for startups with a superior product, scalable business, with a large market and passionate entrepreneurial team. Founders that are devoted and confident, show intelligent integration between knowledge and resources, and aiming for a quick break through. We love founders that are embracing the addictive rawness of entrepreneurship, and transcends this endorphin filled energy to their team to achieve amazing things together.

+

Investment & Incubation

Several of our portfolio companies relocate to our Incubation space in Shanghai, Lujiazui. JadeValue provides startups with free prime office for 6 months, access to our large global network of media, investors and Fintech professionals. They will also have exclusive access to our great in-house team with expertise in everything from e.g. data analytics, recruitment, fund raising, government affairs to graphic design. By removing everyday barriers, we assist the startups to speed up their growth and increase their likelihood of gaining real traction.

General Manager

He is also a VP in CashBUS, a market-leading mobile consumer financial service provider in China, founded in 2014. He is a graduate from Stockholm University, and has also studied at Fudan University in Shanghai and NNMU in South Africa.

Mus Tang

Investment, GR & VGM

Mus Tang is an associate Vice President of JadeValue and CashBUS, working on maintaining and expanding JadeValue’s investment and government relationships. He is also a serial entrepreneur, a grounded business strategist and an angel investor with more than ten years’ experience from internet startups. Prior, he also worked with Huawei as a Key Account Manager in Ghana.

Frank Pei

Investment & BD

Frank Pei is our Director of Investment & BD, responsible for startup scouting, and VC/PE network development. Steve Shao

BD & AI / Big Data Advisor

He holds a Ph.D. degree in electrical engineering from Tsinghua University.
