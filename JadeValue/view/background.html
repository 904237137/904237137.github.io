BACKGROUND

China has the largest FinTech market in the world, with a highly tech-savvy population and leading innovation.

Inclusive finance will be one of the key drivers for the future of China’s economic growth.

JadeValue is powered by powered by CashBUS, the leading Fintech micro-consumer finance company in China. Founded by Tang Yang in 2014, CashBUS offering inclusive micro consumer loans via a single payment and installment loan option. The company’s credit scoring is automated and based on world-class proprietary technology, leveraging big data and advanced machine learning algorithms.

With a fast growing tech native middle class, but still underserved general public in terms of access to traditional financial services, there are major opportunities for innovative visionary Fintech players to fill this gap and provide more inclusive customer centered financial services.

The enormous boom of the Chinese Internet giants through e.g. mobile payments, e-commerce and O2O solutions, China has been recognized as a leading Fintech hub. But the fact remains that a large part of the population remains unbanked, lacking credit history and have difficulties obtaining loans with reasonable rates.

JadeValue aims to invest and incubate Fintech startups with the vision and potential to have a large impact on financial innovation, and create a more inclusive future. Started by Fintech entrepreneurs for Fintech entrepreneurs.
